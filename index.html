Yasutaka Koike-Mori

PhD candidate in Economics

University of California, Los Angeles

I am a 5th year PhD student in economics at UCLA. I will be on the job market during the 2024-2025 academic year.

Prior to UCLA, I worked as an economist at the Bank of Japan’s Research and Statistics Department, where I was involved in macroeconomic modeling and served as the head of the consumption sector team for the quarterly economic forecast prepared for the Monetary Policy Meetings.
